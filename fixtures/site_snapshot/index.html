Deli Brand Family
Deli Office

Deli Office is one of the leading bulk office stationery suppliers in the world, providing the global market with affordable, trustworthy, one-stop products and solutions. Deli brings quality and efficiency to every office.

School Stationery

Deli School is a brand that focuses on design, affordability, and user experience for all students. Its goal is to support children’s development with inspiring solutions and make studying more enjoyable.

Nursing is committed to bringing personality, bold design, and the finest quality to office environments through iconic and trendy products that reflect its consumer's attitudes and spirit.

Agnite is a comprehensive sports supply solution brand targeting students and sports enthusiasts. By providing unique styles, and reliable and cost-effective products, Agnite aims to deliver phenomenal sports experiences and enhance user performance.

Deli Plus brings bespoke office furniture products & services to all work scenarios. Leveraging its robust multi-channel resources, Deli Plus creates exquisite quality products with modern European designs and is deeply committed to providing customers with innovative and empowering furniture.

Deli Tools

The Deli Tools brand delivers a full range of hand tools, power tools, and supportive services with guaranteed quality and value. Deli Tools is a reliable mate for workers and DIYers alike.

Jishi

JISHI is a professional group-buying platform for companies and governments and features transparent, shared, and easy-to-use supply services. With strong resource integration based on Deli Group's nationwide logistics network, JISHI is pushing the limits and continuously improving group-buying efficiency.

Deli Genius

As an educational brand owned by Deli Group Co., Ltd. China, Deli Genius is committed to helping parents explore the potential of children, and create a wonderful infinite childhood. Let children grow together with Deli Genius!

Dmast is a Sino-French joint venture brand between Deli and Pébéo. The brand is about self-expression in an adventure led by passion that relys on the journey itself.
